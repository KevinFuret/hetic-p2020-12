BLOCKS Dashboard

Control your favorite studio software through BLOCKS Dashboard.
Allows easy integration between BLOCKS and your DAWs and plug-ins.

Customize BLOCKS for your workflow.

  • Fine-tune your performance

    Through BLOCKS Dashboard you can tweak the settings you use most when performing and producing.

  • Build your own app for BLOCKS

    The most hackable music creation system ever made, BLOCKS is a platform for app creation. Through Dashboard you can easily script your own programs for BLOCKS.

  • Configure an open-ended interface

    Tweak sounds through 5 macro controls chosen by ROLI sound designers. Macros differ for each sound.

  • Reverb and Delay effects

    Blocks become open-ended MIDI control surfaces through BLOCKS Dashboard. Customize the LED-illuminated Lightpad Block and more.
