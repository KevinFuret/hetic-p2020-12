Equator Player

A desktop sound player with over 200 sounds. Richly textured, high-resolution sounds come straight from Equator, ROLI’s synthesis and sound engine.

Tailor made sounds and tools to make your own

  • Sound selector

    Browse presets by name or by instrument.

  • Preset descriptions and tags

    Learn about how to perform with a preset, and arrange your sound library through tags.

  • Macro controls

    Tweak sounds through 5 macro controls chosen by ROLI sound designers. Macros differ for each sound.

  • Reverb and Delay effects

    Add reverb and delay effects to your sound. Toggle them on and off in the Effects panel.

  • Seaboard Block visualisation

    Visualize touch on the keyboard surface of a Seaboard Block.
